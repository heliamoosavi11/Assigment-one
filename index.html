Project Idea

My Project idea is a Buy now pay layer for alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks at bars and restaurants.Based on my research this Idea already exist for buying liquor by the bottle at liquor stores but not individual drinks on a night out. This will be an app where people can purchase individual drinks by paying the ¼ of the amount upfront and the rest over a weekly payment plan.

Motivation

Currently there are so many Buy now pay later options available for retail, electronics, flights and food but there has not been an option for liquor and alcohol Going for a night out could be very expensive and not everyone can afford to pay $20+ for one drink so this app allows individuals to enjoy their night on a budget friendly matter.

Description

The way this app would work is by generating a barcoded voucher worth of $150. The individual is allowed to purchase 1 drink at once. Once the drinks are purchased there will be a timer set on the app for a waiting period of one hour to purchase the next drink. The timer allows the drinks consumed to be spaced out. There will also be a limit of 4 drinks per night as it is recommended by Australian health government that a healthy adult should only consume 4 drinks in one day.

Tools

•iOS programming abilities in Swift •Kotlin •Jenkins, Artifactory, GitHub, Jira, Confluence, Bitrise, intelliJ and Appcentre .

Outcome

I think this app could be very successful as there has not been a payment plan system for alcoholic drinks.It can also be an affordable way for people to enjoy their nightlife without spending too much money at once.
